# 🚀 InsightGenAI – AI-Powered Automated Data Analytics Platform

> **From Raw Data to Business Insights — Powered by AI**

InsightGenAI is an end-to-end AI-powered Data Analytics Platform that automates the complete analytics workflow—from data ingestion and preprocessing to AI-generated business insights and conversational analytics. It enables analysts to spend less time on repetitive data preparation and more time solving real business problems.

# ⭐ Why I Built InsightGenAI

While working on multiple data analytics projects, I noticed that every project followed the same repetitive workflow:

📂 Import Data
🧹 Clean Data
📊 Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
📈 Build Visualizations
📝 Interpret Results
💡 Write Business Insights

Although these tasks are essential, they consume a significant portion of an analyst's time before meaningful analysis even begins.

This made me ask a simple question:

> **"What if AI could automate repetitive analytical tasks while allowing Data Analysts to focus on solving business problems?"**

That question became the inspiration behind **InsightGenAI**.

The goal isn't to replace Data Analysts.

The goal is to build an intelligent AI assistant that accelerates their workflow, improves productivity, and helps them make better business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

InsightGenAI automates the complete analytics pipeline by enabling users to:

* Upload CSV and Excel datasets
* Connect directly to PostgreSQL databases
* Assess data quality
* Automatically clean and preprocess data
* Detect outliers
* Perform feature engineering
* Generate interactive EDA dashboards
* Produce AI-powered business insights
* Identify root causes
* Generate actionable recommendations
* Ask questions using Natural Language through an AI chatbot

Everything happens within a single application.

## 3️⃣ Data Health Score

>  **<img width="898" height="360" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1c9208e6-9d82-4efc-8a9f-adfe6c4936d5" />**


One of the unique features of InsightGenAI is the **Data Health Score**.

Instead of manually inspecting hundreds of columns, analysts instantly understand the overall quality of their dataset.

The score is calculated using multiple quality indicators such as:

* Missing Data
* Duplicate Records
* Invalid Data Types
* Constant Columns
* Data Consistency
* Data Completeness

### Why is the Data Health Score important?

High-quality data is the foundation of accurate analytics.

Poor-quality data can produce misleading dashboards, inaccurate business insights, and unreliable machine learning models.

The Data Health Score acts as a quality checkpoint, helping analysts determine whether the dataset is ready for analysis or requires further cleaning.

## Statistical Summary

> **<img width="853" height="370" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/28881bd2-8e05-4c5f-836b-08af5ab232fa" />
**

Before diving into visualizations and AI-generated insights, InsightGenAI automatically generates a comprehensive **Statistical Summary** of the dataset.

The summary provides key descriptive statistics for numerical features, including:

- Count
- Mean
- Median
- Standard Deviation
- Minimum & Maximum Values
- Quartiles (25%, 50%, 75%)
- Variance
- Skewness
- Kurtosis

This allows analysts to quickly understand the distribution, spread, and characteristics of the data, identify potential anomalies, and validate data quality before performing deeper analysis.

The statistical summary serves as the foundation for reliable Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and helps ensure that subsequent insights are based on a well-understood dataset.
